About Me

Emanuel Azcona is a first year Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at Northwestern University in Evanston, Illinois. Before coming to Northwestern he completed his undergraduate studies at New York University: B.S. Electrical Engineering (2017). His undergraduate thesis/capstone focused on the development of supervised machine learning models to predict playoff contention in the National Basketball Association (NBA).

His research interests are now focused on special topics crossing over signal/image processing and machine learning. Before undertaking doctoral studies in 2017, Emanuel worked as a lead instructor for the Girls Who Code organization (2017), research assistant at New York University (2015-2016), and intern at John Deere (2014).
